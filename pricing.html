FAQ

Questions, answered.

Can I try Lumen before paying?
Yes. The Starter plan is free forever for up to 3 users, and every Pro feature is available as a 14-day trial — no credit card required. You can upgrade, downgrade, or cancel at any time.
How does the annual billing work?
Annual billing is charged once per year and saves you 20% versus monthly. You can switch between monthly and annual at any time, and we'll prorate the difference.
Which accounting tools does Lumen integrate with?
Lumen connects to NetSuite, QuickBooks, Xero, Sage Intacct, Stripe, Brex, Mercury, and Ramp out of the box. Enterprise customers can also build custom integrations via our API.
Is my financial data secure?
Yes. All data is encrypted at rest with AES-256 and in transit with TLS 1.2+. We're SOC 2 Type II certified and GDPR ready, with SSO/SAML and SCIM available on Enterprise.
How long does onboarding take?
Most teams are live in under a week. Start from a template, import your existing checklist, and connect your ledger — no code or consultants required.
What happens when I hit the user limit on Starter?
You'll get an in-app prompt to upgrade to Pro, which removes all user, workflow, and entity limits. Your data and workflows carry over seamlessly — nothing is lost.
